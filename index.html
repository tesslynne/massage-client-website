What is Reiki?

Reiki treats the whole person including body, emotions, mind and spirit creating many beneficial effects that include relaxation and feelings of peace, security and wellbeing. Reiki is a simple laying-on-of-hands healing methodology. It is the channeling of higher energy through an individual, allowing them to impart that energy as a profound act of healing, opening, and releasing. This energy supports and heals the emotional, mental and physical body and can restore the natural energy flow, clear blockages and allow the body to heal on its own - bringing new insight into your spiritual and mental well being. Each healing session is unique, and every individual will experience Reiki differently. Reiki does not adhere to any dogma, and practitioners need not embrace any specific belief system. Reiki healers come from all walks of life and many different philosophical and spiritual backgrounds.

Reiki has enriched Cat's life immeasurably, and it is with joy and a sense of service she shares reiki on the Central Coast. She also offers therapeutic reiki sessions for pets.

About Cat Rhyne

Physiological & Spiritual Healer

Growing up on Galveston bay in Texas and a Gemini to boot, Cat is definitely a water baby. Whether living on St. Simons Island in Georgia or in Cambria in California, her natural habitat is small-town coastal life. Before getting her massage license in 1994, Cat lived life to the fullest, moving through marriage, motherhood and widowhood. As a drug and alcohol counselor, she developed her healing touch more fully, though all her life she has been a source of comfort for creatures big and small.

She loves to travel the world and has learned from masters in Thailand, New Zealand and beyond. After many years with Reiki, she finds that her fingers communicate visual images to her from which she starts to see clients' physical/emotional concerns manifest. She really feels that she has help from these Angels and guides. Cat admits, with a humble laugh, that she sounds pretty 'airy fairy'. "You can't imagine what a difference this is from how I grew up...It took me so long to trust that this is real. I have an agreement with the Angels that I will share whatever they show me. It is really the person's own belief systems and their guides that talk to me."
